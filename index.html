Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes, we follow strict security protocols and never store your attachments on our servers. All data remains in your Google account. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.

Does it work with Google Workspace?

Absolutely! Gmail Attachment Organizer works seamlessly with both personal Gmail accounts and Google Workspace accounts.

How much does it cost?

The extension is completely free to install and use. We offer premium features for power users at a small monthly subscription.

What happens if I uninstall the extension?

Your Gmail remains unchanged. The extension only creates an organizational layer over your existing attachments without modifying them.

Does it affect Gmail performance?

The extension is designed to be lightweight and has minimal impact on Gmail's performance.

How often does it scan for new attachments?

The extension automatically scans for new attachments whenever you receive new emails, ensuring your attachment library is always up to date.
